PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: January 19, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

At PVDSpot, we believe that privacy is a fundamental right. Our tools are designed to work without ever needing to store your personal files or sensitive information on our disks.

01. Data Collection

We do not collect personal identification information (PII). When you use our digital utilities, the processing happens locally in your browser (Client-Side) or via secure ephemeral memory that is wiped immediately after the task is completed.

02. How We Use Information

Any non-identifiable technical data (such as browser type or page visit duration) is used strictly for:

  • Monitoring and maintaining the stability of our systems.
  • Improving the user experience and tool performance.
  • Analyzing aggregate traffic patterns to optimize server locations.

03. Cookies & Tracking

We may use minimal cookies to remember your preferences (like Dark Mode). We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without losing core functionality of our tools.

04. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect PVDSpot. Since we do not store your files, the risk of data breaches involving your content is virtually non-existent. For tools that require server interaction, we use SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit.

05. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site you visit.

06. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at pvdspot@gmail.com.
